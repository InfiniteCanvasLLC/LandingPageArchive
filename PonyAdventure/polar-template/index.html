Play God in a living, breathing world of magical creatures

Will you choose the path of good, or embrace evil?

In a world populated by magical ponies, unicorns, and other mythical creatures - you must forge your own path.

Form alliances, build friendships, and prevail against evil. Or reject your brethren, and become a powerful tyrant.

Features

Dozens of quests build friendships...or destroy them

Cooperate and compete with the other ponies in your village in heart-stopping minigames and other challenges.

Work together for the greater good, or lie & cheat looking out only for yourself.

Customize & train your pony

As you win challenges, you get stronger. Upgrade your traits like courage and loyalty. Or you can improve your strength and speed if you prefer a physical approach.

Groom your horse the way you want, your imagination is the only limit. Color, accessories, hairstyle, and more!

Dynamic & Exciting Relationships

Your relationships with the creatures of the land is of utmost importance. You exist in a bustling world full of life.

Open world gameplay

Discover new quests and minigames as you travel the land. Countless hours of gameplay to be had.

Bring your friends into your world

Send your pony into your friend's villages and co-operate or hinder, it's up to you!
